Welcome

Without A Trace is your one-stop-shop for contact tracing the current Covid-19 pandemic. This web application was designed to better keep track of where people could be spreading the virus and when. By logging in your recent visits, Without A Trace will cross examine the times and dates in order to inform you of who you might have come in to contact with.
